Signature recovery (Section 4.3, step 7 — recoverTypedDataAddress): Pre-verifying the signature off-chain before submitting sounds smart, but it adds complexity and latency for something the chain will reject anyway. Pre-checking bot USDC balance (Section 10.3): Same logic. Adds ~200ms latency and a balanceOf RPC call. If the bot has no funds, the tx reverts. Gas is sponsored so there's no cost. The revert is actually clearer feedback.

Dynamic product URL webhook (Section 5.4): A webhook that CreditClaw fires during the settlement response to get a dynamic URL from the seller... this introduces timeout risk and significantly complicates the settlement flow. Definitely a v2 thing, and the plan acknowledges that.
